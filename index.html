About Us

MCDF is a charity foundation based on helping the less privileged and uneducated.

On the educational level

The foundation has proven itself in the field of education to be very selfless and generous. It has gone out of its way to ensure that more youths are educated and their future bright. various acheivement made by this foundation includes:

  • Provision of scholarships to numerous students .
  • visits to IDP(internally displaced persons) camps .
  • Training programs for teachers and students.
  • 2 million Naira expended through self funding in a space of 2 years.

Nigeria has the highest number of out-of-school children with 10.5 million out of the cummulative 20 million unenrolled children.No nation can achieve economic prosperity without a sound, inclusive and functional educational system. The security and stability of the country, to a large extent, depends on its ability to provide functional education to its citizens.

On the enterprenuership level

The foundtion beleives that to be successful, one must couple education with enterprenuership. With enough diligence and provision success is guaranteed. This notion birthed the creation of a skill acquisition program to enable youths and adults acquire a technical and inovative skill that will alliviate the situation of unemployment in the country. acheivement made by the foundation on these grounds are:

  • Creation of a skill program to equip youths on various enterprenuer skills.
  • Provision of materials.
  • employing the experience of professionals in different areas of enterprenuership.
  • Provision of certificates to diligent and hard-working students.

About the founder

As founder of Mercy Heart Foundation (MHF), Mercy A. Balami is a Nigerian philanthropist with five years of providing quality education, socio-economic empowerment and community livelihoods in Nigeria for out-of-school children, She is an advocate of the sustainable development goals. She launched a project "EDUCATION FIRST" which has taken over 50 students off the streets and offered them free education. She beleives that for any nation to grow, her young generation needs to be groomed with a strong fibre of literacy in their growing years, to this she has made a long term commitment through self-funding by ensuring that students get the best quality education reguardless.
